More About Me

Hi!
I'm Yehonatan.

I am all about good quality functional design.

You will most likely find me tinkering with some new materials, planning and building things, or working out.

I currently work at i24NEWS as a Graphics and Workflow Manager.
I love working with people, tailoring graphic scenes and templates to their needs.

After three years of experience in the field, I consider myself an expert problem solver.

I studied Industrial Design at Ariel University and graduated with honors.
Although I focused mainly on physical products during my studies, what I most enjoyed was the graphic design component, creating and communicating an experience for the user of the product.
Today I combine my education as a designer, UI/UX certification, and experience in visual communication to dream and create.
